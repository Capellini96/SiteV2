HEAD TO HEAD

The simplest bet as the name says it’s 1vs1, the creator of the bets decides the parameters. In these bets there is the possibility to pick a 3rd party to settle the result as long as both participants agree.

MULTIPLAYER

These bets suppress the need of a high capital bettor that wants to bet big bucks, allowing multiple users to join his bet on the opposite side to ensure he gets matched for the total of his capital.

PREDICTION MARKETS

Users will be able to buy and sell shares on each side of the possible outcome, until the market is resolved. The price of the shares changes dinamically according to how likely the market is going to settle.

NON-INDEXED

The user will be able to bet on any kind of subject. You can bet with your friend if it’s going to rain tomorrow, who drinks a beer faster, literally anything. The outcome is decided by 1 or 3 “judges”, chosen by the bettors.
